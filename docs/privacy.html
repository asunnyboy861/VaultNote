VaultNote Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

TL;DR: VaultNote stores all your data locally on your device. We never collect, transmit, or have access to your notes. Your data is yours — always.

1. Data Collection

VaultNote does NOT collect any personal data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Crash reports
  • Any content you create within the app

2. Data Storage

All your notes and data are stored exclusively on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms:

  • Core Data: Notes are stored locally in the app's sandbox
  • Keychain: Encryption keys are stored in the iOS Keychain
  • AES-256-GCM Encryption: All note content is encrypted at rest

3. iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud Sync, your encrypted notes are synced via your personal iCloud account. VaultNote uses CloudKit with end-to-end encryption. We never have access to your synced data.

4. Biometric Authentication

VaultNote uses Face ID / Touch ID via the iOS LocalAuthentication framework. Biometric data never leaves your device's Secure Enclave. We do not collect or store biometric data.

5. Third-Party Services

VaultNote does NOT integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. There are no SDKs from Facebook, Google, or other tracking companies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties. Since we do not collect data in the first place, there is nothing to share.

7. Data Retention

All data remains on your device until you delete it. Uninstalling the app removes all data. We retain no server-side copies.

8. Children's Privacy

VaultNote is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Your Rights

Since we collect no data, there is nothing to access, modify, or delete on our servers. You have full control over your data on your device.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
