Frequently Asked Questions

How is my data protected?
All your notes are encrypted with AES-256-GCM encryption before being stored on your device. Encryption keys are stored in the iOS Keychain with hardware-level protection. Even if someone gains physical access to your device, they cannot read your notes without your biometric authentication or passcode.
Where is my data stored?
All data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud Sync, encrypted notes are synced through your personal iCloud account using CloudKit with end-to-end encryption. We never have access to your data.
What happens if I forget my passcode?
VaultNote uses your device's biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) or device passcode. If you can unlock your iPhone, you can unlock VaultNote. There is no separate password to forget.
Does VaultNote collect any of my data?
No. VaultNote does not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your data. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. Your notes stay on your device, encrypted.
Can I recover deleted notes?
Once a note is deleted from VaultNote, it cannot be recovered. This is by design to ensure your data privacy. We recommend using iCloud Sync as a backup mechanism.
Is there a subscription?
No. VaultNote is a one-time purchase. Pay once, use forever. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no hidden fees.
